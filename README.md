# Uber-Data-Analysis-Tableau
### Business Problem  
Analyzed Uber ride data to identify demand patterns, peak hours, and high-revenue locations for driver allocation and operational efficiency.  

### Key Questions  
* What are the peak hours and days for ride demand?  
* Which locations generate the highest ride volume and revenue?  
* How does demand vary across time and location?  

### Tools & Technologies  
* Tableau – interactive dashboards  
* Excel – data preprocessing  

### Dataset  
* Uber ride data including pickup times, locations, and trip counts  

### Analysis & Insights  
* Evening peak hours had highest demand  
* Airports and business districts generated higher revenue per ride  
* Weekends showed different demand patterns than weekdays  

### Recommendations  
* Allocate more drivers during peak periods  
* Optimize surge pricing during high-demand times  
* Improve driver positioning in high-demand areas
